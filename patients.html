We help patients like you navigate the complexities of participating in a clinical trial.

My doctor has told me I have kidney disease. What should I do?

There are many factors that cause kidney disease. Talk with your doctor to understanding fully what kind of kidney disease you have. Understanding all your options will help guide you to what is right for you.

Should I enroll in clinical trial?

Understanding your options is the biggest hurdle. Once you know how your kidney disease is treated, deciding if you should enroll in a clinical trial will be easier.

Will a clinical trial help me?

Treatment with investigational new drugs may or may not help you. Participating in clinical trials does bring new information that may be helpful for the treatment of chronic kidney disease
